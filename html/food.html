FOOD RECIPES

Vegetable Noodles

  1. TAKE A PANE
  2. ADD CHOP TOMATOS
  3. ADD RED CHILLI POWDER AND SALT
  4. TURN ON THE FLAME
  5. ADD CHICKEN POWDER
  6. ADD CHILLI SAUCE AND SOYA SAUCE
  7. ADD VINIGER IN IT
  8. ADD VEGETABLES LIKE:
    • CAPSICUM
    • CARROT
    • ONION
    • BEANS
    • GREEN CHILLI
    • CABBAGE
    • PEAS
  9. ADD BOILED NOODLES IN IT AND MIX WELL
  10. ENJOY IT!

Choclate Cake

  1. TAKE A BOWL
  2. ADD TWO EGGS AND BLEND
  3. ADD HALF CUP GRIND SUGER IN IT AND BLEND WELL
  4. ADD 5 TABLE SPOON OIL
  5. ADD ONE CUP FLOUR AND BAKING POWDER IN IT
  6. ADD ONE CUP FLOUR AND BAKING POWDER IN IT
  7. ADD VANILLA ESSENCE
  8. ADD COCOPOWDER AND CHOCLATE CHIPS IN IT AND MIX WELL
  9. TAKE A PANE COVER WITH BAKING PAPER
  10. ADD CAKE BATTER IN IT
  11. PUT IT ON FLAME AND COVER IT
  12. AFTER 15 MINUTES REMOVE IT FROM FLAME
  13. CUT IT FROM CENTRE AND ADD CHOCLATE SPREAD IN IT
  14. COVER ALL SIDES WITH WHIPPED CREAM
  15. ADD CHOCLAT CHIPS ON UPER SIDES
  16. ENJOY IT!

Lotus dessert

  1. TAKE A BOWL
  2. ADD CREAM IN BOWL
  3. ADD LOTUS SPREAD IN IT AND MIX WELL
  4. ADD CONDENSE MILK IN IT
  5. FOUR TABLE SPOON MILK IN IT
  6. MIX WELL
  7. TAKE ANOTHER BOWL
  8. TAKE PLAN CAKE
  9. TAKE A HALF CUP OF MILK
  10. TALK SLICE OF PLAN CAKE
  11. DIP IN MILK AND PUT ON BOWL
  12. SAME AS FILL BOWL WITH CAKE
  13. ADD LOTUS BATTER IN IT
  14. ADD LOTUS BATTER IN IT
  15. COVER IT WITH LOTUS BUSCUIT
  16. ENJOY IT!
